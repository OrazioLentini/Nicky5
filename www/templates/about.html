Nicky3 is an app that has been designed to tackle the needs of today’s conferences and events.

Nicky3 was created by Nariel LLC, a web development company which is experienced in working trade show trade shows for more years than they want to count. Nicky3 is now part of the team. Fully customizable to have as much or as little as is required.

Nicky3 can provide the basics:
• Registration
• Directories
• Schedules
• Maps
• Event Information

But she can also do much more than that:
• User Interaction at Live Meetings
• Social Media Connections
• Gamification
• New Product Showcases
• Quizzes, Surveys, and Polls
• Learning Modules
• Photo Galleries & Videos

Nicky3 has no ego. If you need her to do more, no problem. She is flexible and willing to change, adapt, and grow just for you. If you would like to learn more on how to get your hands on your own customized app.
